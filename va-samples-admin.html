2. Internet Research Summary

Research Topic: Top 5 Virtual Assistant Tools for 2024
Research Summary Date: January 15, 2024 Source: Multiple industry blogs and reviews Key Findings: 1. Trello - Project Management - Free plan available - Easy to use with drag-and-drop interface - Integrates with Google Workspace - Best for: Task tracking and team collaboration 2. Calendly - Scheduling - Free tier for individual users - Automated booking with timezone detection - Reduces back-and-forth emails - Best for: Meeting scheduling 3. Slack - Communication - Free for small teams - Organized channels by topic - File sharing and search capabilities - Best for: Team communication 4. Google Workspace - Productivity Suite - Docs, Sheets, Slides, Gmail - Real-time collaboration - Cloud storage (15GB free) - Best for: Document creation and storage 5. Canva - Design - Free version with templates - Social media graphics and presentations - Brand kit feature for consistency - Best for: Basic graphic design needs Recommendation: For a new VA starting out, Google Workspace + Trello + Calendly provides a solid foundation for productivity.
